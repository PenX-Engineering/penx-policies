PenX Data Use Policy

Effective Date: [April 14,2026]

At PenX, we are committed to protecting your data and using it responsibly. This Data Use Policy explains how we use the information collected through our platform.

1. Our Commitment

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

2. How We Use Data

We use your data strictly for internal purposes, including:

  • Improving matching and user experience
  • Enhancing app performance and reliability
  • Analyzing usage trends to improve features
  • Ensuring user safety and preventing abuse

3. Limited Use of Third-Party Services

To operate and improve PenX, we use trusted third-party service providers such as:

  • Google Play Services (for billing and payments)
  • Firebase services (for analytics, authentication, and database operations)

These providers act as data processors and only process data on our behalf to support the functionality of the app. They are not permitted to sell or use your data for independent marketing purposes.

4. No Data Selling or Advertising Use

PenX does not sell your personal data and does not share your data with third parties for advertising, profiling, or resale.

5. Data Minimization

We only collect data that is necessary to provide and improve our services.

6. User Control

You have control over your data. You may update or delete your information within the app or request deletion by contacting us.

7. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Data Use Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
